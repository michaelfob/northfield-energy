Common Questions

Simple answers before you apply.

Do I pay anything upfront?

For qualifying approved projects, the structure is designed to allow clients to proceed with no upfront cost. Final terms depend on eligibility, approval, system design and survey.

How long does this take?

Assessment starts on day one. The savings report follows around day three, survey around day seven, application around day ten, approval around days 15–21, and installation is typically targeted around day 30 subject to survey, approval and installer availability.

Who installs the system?

Installation is handled by certified commercial installation partners.

What does Northfield Energy charge?

Costs are explained transparently before you proceed with any project.
